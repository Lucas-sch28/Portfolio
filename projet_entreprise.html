Codage PowerShell

Mise en place de scripts pour automatiser la gestion de l'infrastructure de l'Active Directory et l'export d'informations.

$users = Get-ADUser -Properties Company, Department, Title, MemberOf, DisplayName -Filter * -Server $AdServer

$groups = Get-ADGroup -Properties * -Filter * -Server $AdServer

$company = $users | Where Company | Sort Company | Select Company, Memberof | Group-object Company #| export-csv $companyCsv -NoTypeInformation -Delimiter ";"

$department = $users | Where department | Sort department | Select department, Memberof #| export-csv $departmentCsv -NoTypeInformation -Delimiter ";"
